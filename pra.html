Kautilya's Arthashastra

LAW SUBJECTS

Kautilya's Arthashastra is an ancient Indian Sanskrit treatise on statecraft, political philosophy, economic policy, and military strategy. It represents one of the most sophisticated political texts of the ancient world.

Traditionally attributed to Chanakya, the Mauryan prime minister, the Arthashastra discusses governance, diplomacy, espionage, taxation, law, and warfare in remarkable depth.
